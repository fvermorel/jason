A witch in love
⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁
This all seemed to simple! Jason went to the room that the King had made available to him. During the night Medea came to meet him. She knew that her father had set a trap for her lover and she was coming to help him, Medea was a magician.
She gave Jason a salve, a rock and a small pouch of powder and explained how to use these items. The next day would be the day of the challenges and she wanted that her lover make it out alive.
Tu as bien compris : si, dans un champ, tu croises un taureau, surtout n’essaie pas de monter dessus. Tu ne sais pas ce qu’il pourrait t’arriver !
The bull
⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁
The furious bull was no ordinary bull. His nostrils breathed fire! But Jason was covered in the magic salve and the fire rolled over his skin without even browning it. He grabbed the bull by the horns and brought him under the yoke. Once calmed, the bull became a normal animal.
Quel beau bébé ! Ahreu Je vais en faire beaucoup d'autres...

The warriors
⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁
Jason now needed to seed the giants’ teeth given to him by the King. Jason tilled a clearing and behind him large furious soldiers appeared, bristling with weapons, as if they had grown like shafts of wheat. Jason threw the stone in between all of them. They rushed one another, and killing each other left our hero safe and sound.
The Dragon
⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁
Finally, he approached the cave. It was guarded by a dragon that had a shell so thick that no sword or arrow could pierce it. Jason blew the powder in its nose. The monster fell asleep right away. Jason entered the cave. It was lit up as if it was in full sunlight because the fleece of a ram was placed on a rock and gleamed like sunlight.
He had achieved his quest! He had retrieved the Golden Fleece!
Hourra ! Victoire ! Vive Cadmos !
Thèbes sera une ville qui abritera beaucoup de héros très célèbres. Le plus fameux d’entre eux, Oedipe, aura un destin tragique que nous vous raconterons dans un prochain livre.
The getaway
⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁⠁
The king of Colchis was furious! Never had he believed that Jason could overcome the obstacles and he couldn’t understand how he did it. He put on a brave face and organized a big party in Jason’s honor. He didn’t want him to return right away to the Argo and he fully intended to kill him during the night.
Medea guessed her father’s thoughts. With her magician skills, she lifted a great fog that obscured the whole palace in darkness. The two lovers took advantage of this to run away in a chariot she had readied.
The king sent his best riders to chase them. As they were ready to catch up with them, Medea threw out her jewelry, her bracelets and even her earrings. The guards stopped to get them and our heroes gained distance from them. They arrived at the Argo where their friends were waiting for them. The guards did not want to attack the Argonauts who then left the port without issues.
